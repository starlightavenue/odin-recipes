Cinnamon Chip Pumpkin Mini Muffins

Quick prep mini muffins to usher in autumn!

This recipe is for delicious pumpkin mini muffins with cinnamon chips for some spice! They're easy to prepare and perfect for a breakfast treat or a snack throughout the day!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 15.25 oz package yellow cake mix
  • 1 15 oz can pumpkin pie mix
  • 1 10 oz package cinnamon chips
