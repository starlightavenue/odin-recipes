Cinnamon Chip Pumpkin Mini Muffins

Quick prep mini muffins to usher in autumn!

This recipe is for delicious pumpkin mini muffins with cinnamon chips for some spice! They're easy to prepare and perfect for a breakfast treat or a snack throughout the day!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 15.25 oz package yellow cake mix
  • 1 15 oz can pumpkin pie mix
  • 1 10 oz package cinnamon chips

Steps

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a mini muffin tin with cooking spray.
  2. Combine cake mix, pumpkin pie mix, and cinnamon chips in a large bowl; mix well with a wooden spoon.
  3. Scoop mixture into muffin tin, filling each cup 3/4 of the way full.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 17 to 20 minutes.
  5. Let cool in tin for 5 minutes, then cool on wire rack for ten minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
