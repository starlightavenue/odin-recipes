Banana Bread

A moist loaf full of banana flavor!

This recipe will help you create the best banana bread you've ever had. It's soft and sweet, and it's going to be your go-to recipe, guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large room-temp eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan with butter.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a large bowl.
  3. Beat butter and brown sugar in a separate large bowl until smooth.
  4. Mash bananas in a third large bowl.
  5. Add eggs to banana mixture and stir until fully combined.
  6. Pour banana/egg mixture into the brown sugar/butter mixture and stir until fully combined.
  7. Pour wet ingredients until dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  8. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  9. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes.
  10. Cool loaf in pan for about 10 minutes and then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Slice and enjoy!
