Banana Bread

A moist loaf full of banana flavor!

This recipe will help you create the best banana bread you've ever had. It's soft and sweet, and it's going to be your go-to recipe, guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large room-temp eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas
